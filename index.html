About LondrCoin

LondrCoin ($LONDR) is a meme-inspired ERC20 token created to celebrate innovation in laundry services, the gig economy, and decentralized rewards. It is designed as a fun, community-driven project that showcases the future of micro-economies.

The gig economy is booming, yet traditional loyalty programs and reward systems have failed to evolve. LondrCoin offers a decentralized way to incentivize service providers and customers alike through blockchain-based rewards.
