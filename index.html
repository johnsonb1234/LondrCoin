About LondrCoin

LondrCoin ($LONDR) is more than just a meme token — it's a movement toward decentralized empowerment. Built on the Ethereum blockchain, LondrCoin fuels innovation in gig work and on-demand services, especially in the laundry sector. By rewarding users, workers, and early adopters, we aim to create a circular economy that is clean, fast, and fair.

Join us as we fold the old economy into a fresh, decentralized future — one wash at a time.
